Hi, I'm a master's student in the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Taiwan University (NTU).

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer graphics and natural language processing.

Currently, I work with Prof. Bing-Yu Chen at the Communication and Multimedia Lab, researching SVG and text-to-image generation.
Previously, I worked as a research assistant for two years at the Natural Language Processing and Sentiment Analysis Lab in the Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica, advised by Prof. Lun-Wei Ku. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering from National Cheng Kung University (NCKU) in 2024, graduating first in my class.
